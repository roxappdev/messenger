P2P Messenger

Secure peer‑to‑peer messaging, audio/video calls, screen sharing, and file transfer. No server storage, fully encrypted.

How to Use

  1. Create a Room – Click "Create Room" to generate a unique link. Share it with your contact.
  2. Connect – Once they open the link, a direct P2P connection is established.
  3. Communicate – Use the tabs to chat, send files, start audio/video calls, or share your screen.
  4. Secure & Private – All data flows directly between you and the peer, encrypted via WebRTC.

No registration, no logs, fully peer‑to‑peer.
